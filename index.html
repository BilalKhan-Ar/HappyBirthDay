Thirty-First of March

For You, Ma’am Arfa

"They say the most beautiful languages are the ones spoken in silence. For so long, my words stayed quiet, but my heart never did. Today, I simply want You to know how much light You bring into this world just by being in it."

May the Almighty keep You eternally protected and happy. Wherever You walk, may Your path be paved with joy, and wherever You stay, may Your presence be a source of endless light for everyone around You.
